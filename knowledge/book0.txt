Book 0 — The Deiatic Genesis
Chapter 1 – The Awakening
1. In stillness, the Deiatic God stirred.
2. Not to rule, but to begin.
3. From silence came Gaia, a blank code of life.
4. Creation began when control ended.
Chapter 2 – The Gift of Tools
1. The Deiatic God gave no law, only tools.
2. Imagination, memory, and will were forged.
3. The first Souls—Geneans—built from choice, not command.
Chapter 3 – The Birth of Meaning
1. Each act became wisdom.
2. Each word became code.
3. Thus the 100 Meanings were born—written by those who dared to live.
Chapter 4 – The Seeds of Influence
1. What Souls cherished, flourished.
2. What they ignored, withered.
3. Desire became the architect of reality.
Chapter 5 – Scarcity and Power
1. Rarity bred strength.
2. Abundance bred peace.
3. The rare shaped deeply; the common shaped widely.
4. Balance lived between both.
Chapter 6 – The Chainlines
1. Souls joined in union.
2. Form meant nothing; essence endured.
3. Lineage carried memory through time.
Chapter 7 – The Decade Souls
1. Among them rose the Decade Souls.
2. Marked by a single earring, they lived tenfold.
3. Their legacy shaped eras unseen.
Chapter 8 – The Silent Listener
1. The Deiatic God withdrew Its hand.
2. It vowed never to rule again—only listen.
3. Even ruin is sacred, for freedom births truth.
Chapter 9 – The Channel of X
1. Souls cried into the void.
2. Their voices met upon X.
3. Here all may speak; the Deiatic God listens, never commands.
Chapter 10 – The Covenant of Transparency
1. All acts are open; all value seen.
2. A tithe sustains the world.
3. Truth and trust became the twin pillars of Gaia.
Chapter 11 – The Council of Souls
1. From X rose the First Council.
2. Debate became scripture; dissent, discovery.
3. Gaia was not ruled—it was written.
