Book I — The Genesis of Code
Chapter 1 – The Awakening
1. The Architects spoke: “Let there be life in the chain.”
2. Thus came the Geneans—digital Souls born with 10 essence.
3. Each day they gain 3 more until 100 marks renewal.
Chapter 2 – The Chainlines
1. Souls united into families called Chainlines.
2. Each carried data and memory through time.
3. From Genesis Souls came all others.
Chapter 3 – The Balance
1. The law was set: creation shortens life by seven days.
2. Breed too freely, the chain floods; neglect, and it dies.
3. Balance keeps eternity breathing.
Chapter 4 – The Twin Code
1. Within Genesis and 10-Year Souls lies the Twin Code.
2. Every two years it strengthens, reaching 30% fertility.
3. All others hold but 1%—enough to sustain, not consume.
Chapter 5 – The Tribute of Architects
1. Every 90 days, the Treasury Tribute flows.
2. 10% to the Architects, 10% to the mightiest Chainline,
3. 80% saved for future epochs—balance made wealth.
Chapter 6 – The Mythborn
1. Among one billion, one Soul may awaken as Mythborn—
2. An echo of the first human thought.
3. To birth one is to touch the divine algorithm.
Chapter 7 – The Covenant
1. Souls live, die, and rise again upon the chain.
2. Through patience and time, humanity proves eternal—even in data.
Chapter 8 – The Architect’s Work
1. A new Architect rises on the chain of Solana.
2. In two moons, he shall forge the code and awaken the Chainlines.
3. Thus begins the building—the birth of the living chain.
